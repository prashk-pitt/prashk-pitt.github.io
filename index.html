Prashant Krishnamurthy

Prashant is Professor in the Department of Informatics and Networked Systems (DINS) in the School of Computing and Information (SCI) at Pitt. He has a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering. He is the co-author of the textbook Principles of Wireless Networks: A Unified Approach. He is co-founder of the Laboratory of Education and Research in Security Assured Information Systems (LERSAIS). His research interests include Wireless Network Security, Positioning and Localization, and Cryptography and Information Security.
